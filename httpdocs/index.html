Welcome to Norm’s Beer & Wine, Vienna, Virginia’s neighborhood beer and wine store!  We offer more than 400 kinds of beer and wines from around the world, all in a casual, friendly atmosphere.

Our beer department includes craft beers from breweries like Dominion, Clipper City, Victory, and Stone, as well as standards from Anheuser-Busch and Miller.  Our imports hail from Germany, Belgium, the UK and more. 

The wine shelves have international selections from the Old World as well as the New World.  You’ll find wines from France, Austria, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Italy, Lebanon, Portugal, Spain, as well as from Argentina, Australia, Chile, and South Africa.  Our domestic wines feature well-knowns as well as special finds from California, Oregon, Washington state, and Virginia.

While you’re stocking up on beer and wine, be sure to check out our gourmet snacks.  We’re proud to feature homegrown gourmet foods from right here in Virginia – including peanuts, Route 11 potato chips and micro-brewed root beer and ginger ale from Dominion.  And of course we carry a nice assortment of cheeses and crackers, cookies, chocolates and hot sauces.
