Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you offer case discounts on beer?
    Yes!  Mix and match four six-packs of beer and receive 10% off the mixed case.  Note:  Items on special will help you receive the discount, but will not be reduced further.


  • Do you offer case discounts on wine?
    Yes!  Mix and match 12 bottles of wine and receive 10% off the mixed case.  Note:  Items on special will help you receive the discount, but will not be reduced further.


  • Can I order something if you don’t normally carry it?
    Yes!  We do special orders all the time.  Please keep in mind, however, that the Commonwealth of Virginia only allows us to buy from licensed Virginia wholesalers.


  • Do you sell kegs?
    Yes!  Call 703-242-0100 to place your order by noon Wednesday to have a keg for the weekend.


  • Do you keep kegs in stock?
    We do not keep kegs in stock to ensure freshness.  However, place your order by noon Wednesday and you can have a keg by the weekend.


  • Do you sell spirits?
    No, only Virginia Alcoholic Beverage Commission (ABC) stores are allowed to sell hard liquor in the Commonwealth of Virginia.


  • Do you sell beer singles?
    Yes!  Pick 6 beer singles from our singles section to try and receive a 10% discount.


  • Do you deliver?
    No sorry, we do not offer delivery.


  • How about mail order?
    Again, sorry, we do not ship.

  • Are gift baskets available?
    We have baskets, and we’ll help you decide what to put inside!
